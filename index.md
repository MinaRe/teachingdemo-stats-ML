# Statistical and Machine Learning
## Course Overview
This course provides a bridge between classical statistical inference and modern machine learning. 

### Audience
The curriculum is tailored for a multidisciplinary cohort of ~200 students (1/3 Business, 1/3 Economics, 1/6 Statistics, 1/6 Info Systems).

### Weekly Agenda at a Glance

| Week | Lecture Topic | Tutorial (Python/Colab) |
|:---|:---|:---|
| 1-4 | Foundations of Inference | Setup & Linear Regularization |
| 5-8 | Non-linear & Ensemble Methods | Random Forests & XGBoost |
| 9-11 | Deep Learning & SGD | Neural Network Architectures |
| **12** | **Reinforcement Learning I** | **MDPs & Bellman Equations** |
| 13 | Reinforcement Learning II | Q-Learning for Decision Making |
| 14 | Causal ML & Ethics | Model Interpretability (SHAP) |
